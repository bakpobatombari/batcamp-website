HOW TO MAKE A TOY CAR

MATERIALS

  • Cardboard paper
  • Four mini tyres
  • Broom Stick
  • super glue
  • scissors

HOW TO MAKE A TOY CAR USING THESE MATERIALS:

  1. Cut the cardboard paper into car parts

    2. clickfor example

  2. Drill a hole through the four mini tyres
  3. cut the broom stick to the length 15cm
  4. cut the down part of the cardboard paper to the length 13cm square
  5. put the broom stick through the downpart of the cardboard paper
  6. Put the broom stick through the mini tyres
  7. After paste the parts together
